# MoodDice
MoodDice is a ridiculously simple app that solves "decision paralysis" by randomly rolling virtual dice to pick your next fun activity based on your current mood. Just pick a mood (Happy, Bored, Stressed, Hungry, Tired, Wild), tap the giant dice button, and get an instant, personalized activity suggestion—like "Dance battle with your pet" or "Eat ice cream from the carton."
​

Why This Wins
Users: Everyone aged 16-45 who scrolls endlessly deciding "what to do next" (500M+ potential users worldwide). Perfect for Gen Z/Millennials with TikTok attention spans craving instant fun.
​

Value Prop: Zero thinking required—tap for 3-second dopamine hit. Turns boredom into mini-adventures. Sticks because it's addictive like TikTok roulette.
